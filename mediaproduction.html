Mr. Philippines 2022

The Filipino Intercollegiate Networking Dialogue District 3 (FIND D3) hosts two cultural pageants every year. New York University’s IFA and Columbia University’s Liga Filipina host the male pageant, Mr. Philippines. This pageant promotes Filipino cultural awareness and charity fundraising. I was part of the planning team of the 2022 show, in which I took on many different tasks to help the production of one of the organization’s biggest shows of the year.
