Albert Unsolicited Redesign

Project Explanation

Albert is a NYU platform in which students can do a multitude of administrative tasks, such as register for classes, pay bills, input personal information, etc.
For my UX Design class, I decided my semester-long project would be an unsolicited redesign of Albert, which I focused on the problem that students don't understand or know of some of the resources within Albert.

Skills

Software: Figma and Google Slides (for UX career-level presentation/deliverables)
UX Research Methods: User Interviews, Usability Testing, surveys

Results

○ NYU students commented that they think the redesign is cleaner compared to the original
○ Tackled terminology that NYU students had trouble with their first year
