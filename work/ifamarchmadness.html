IFA March Madness

Project Explanation

During my time as Social Media Chair for NYU's Filipino club, my biggest project was "Filipino Food March Madness." In partnership with the Cultural Chair of our E-board, we hosted March Madness, where our Instagram audience voted on their favorite Filipino foods.

The Cultural Chair organized the food selections and their informative prompts, while I had the role of designing and posting.

The Bracket

Designed using Adobe Photoshop and InDesign
I updated and posted the bracket to Instagram Stories weekly.

The Daily Voting

Designed using Adobe Photoshop and InDesign
Uploaded to Instagram Stories daily.

Results

We saw an increase in Instagram engagement - a consistent voting count of 40-60 daily, as well as many DMs of passionate voters' opinions.
The month ended in a large food event where the winner was announced. About 30-40 members showed up to eat and argue their opinion.
