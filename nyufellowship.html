NYU Experience Design Fellowship

Role Description
As a student, I worked in the Marketing and Communications department as a Student Experience Design Fellow. The fellowship is made of a few students who study design at NYU. We give feedback to and design marketing and communication initiatives at NYU.

Following are some of the many marketing and communication initiatives I worked on as a part of my 2 years in this role:
Meet NYU articles

NYU Shuttle Guide Graphic

The Email Audit
Did quality and data check using Google Sheets and a bit of Javascript to collect data on the average large amount of mass emails sent to NYU students
Grad School Email Marketing Comparative Analysis
Two other Student Design Fellows and I analyzed several graduate school email marketing methods to help plan the ideal email marketing campaign for prospective students for NYU Wagner. I presented on email design and information while my peers focused on subject lines and timing.
NYU "Meet Us on the Square" Animation
Created an animation to be played on a projector throughout an NYU admissions' event using Figma and Adobe Photoshop
