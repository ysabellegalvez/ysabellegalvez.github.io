Albert Unsolicited Redesign

Albert is a NYU website in which students can do a lot of things, such as register for classes, pay bills, input personal information, etc.

Over my time at NYU, I've had many conversations with both students and faculty (as a part of my job at NYU) about the troubles of Albert.

For my UX Design class, I decided my semester-long project would be to do an unsolicited redesign of Albert, which I focused on the problem that students don't understand or know of some of the resources within Albert.

Software: Figma and Google slides

UX Research Methods: User Interviews, Usability Testing, surveys
