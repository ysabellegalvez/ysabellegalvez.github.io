Wolven

Role Description
I was a Design and Social Media Intern at Wolven, a South-Asian owned sustainable clothing brand. Few months after my internship ended and I graduated, I was hired as a Freelance Graphic Designer.
Wholesale Catalogs
Updated wholesale catalogs which involved selecting E-commerce media, updating product descriptions and pricing, and organizing the products by print and style.

Software: Adobe InDesign
Instagram story campaigns
I was given the assignment to design a week's worth of Instagram stories that promoted product launches. I picked out the best photos from photoshoots, wrote copy that appeal to Wolven's audience and brand voice, and came up with the designs. These stories got 300-400 views.

Software: Figma
Email Campaign design
Designed visuals for Klaviyo emails using Figma. Provided copy and CTAs and designed and selected media following that.
Also introduced to Klaviyo - email campaign analytics and A/B testing, and SMS campaigns
Event Graphics
Designed QR code graphics for events using Figma. I also generated QR codes as well.
